Building & Construction

We cover the design, construction, and maintenance of the physical and naturally built environment, including public works such as roads, bridges, dams, water and sewerage systems, and pipelines. We are dedicated to work in this sector, and may specialise in particular types of infrastructure.

Maintenance and Repairs

Skilled at a wide range of repairs. These tasks include trade skills, repair work, maintenance work, are both interior and exterior. Specifically, these jobs could be light plumbing jobs such as fixing a leaky toilet or light electric jobs such as changing a light fixture or bulb.

Painting & Gardening Services

Enhancing interior and exterior to achieve a healthier and more aesthetically pleasing environment for the people using the space. We plan, coordinate, and manages such enhancement projects. including space planning, site inspections, and execution of the designs

Electricty & Welding

Specializing in electrical wiring of buildings, transmission lines, stationary machines, and installation of new electrical components or the maintenance and repair of old electrical infrastructure.

Plumbing

Paving & Tiling

Preparing work areas on floors and walls by removing old tiles, adhesive and grout; filling gaps, crack and holes; and ensuring all surfaces are clean for laying out work while measuring and marking surfaces.
